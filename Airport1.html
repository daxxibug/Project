Naples International Airport (NAP)

Official Name: Capodichino Airport"

Serving as the main gateway to Southern Italy, Naples Airport is the best entry point for visitors heading to the Amalfi Coast, Pompeii, and Capri. It has seen rapid growth in recent years and is known for being very close to the city center (only about 6 km away)

Venice Marpo Polo Airport (VCE)

Official Name: Aerpoporto di Venezia Marco Polo

Named after the famous Venetian explorer, this airport is located on the mainland but offers a unique arrival experience where travelers can take a water taxi or Alilaguna boat directly to the historic center of Venice. It is the primary international airport for the Veneto region.

Catania–Fontanarossa Airport (CTA)

Official Name: Vincenzo Bellini Airport"

This is the busiest airport on the island of Sicily. It is a major hub for domestic flights within Italy and serves travelers visiting Mount Etna and the eastern side of the island. It is named after the famous composer Vincenzo Bellini, who was born in Catania.
