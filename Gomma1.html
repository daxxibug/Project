Automobile (Private/Rental Cars)

Italy is the land of Fiat and Alfa Romeo. While luxury cars (like Ferrari or Lamborghini) are famous, most Italians drive compact hatchbacks for easier parking.

Scooter/Motorino

The iconic Vespa or Lambretta. These are the most popular way to navigate heavy traffic in dense cities like Naples or Rome.

Ape Piaggio

A unique, small three-wheeled commercial vehicle often seen in narrow village streets or used for light deliveries.
