Alpina Dolomites

Located on the Alpe di Siusi, this hotel was built following "ClimaHotel" standards to protect the fragile Alpine environment.

Key Facts:

  • Region: Trentino-Alto Adige (Dolomites), Italy
  • Certification: ClimaHotel/CasaClima
  • Primary Sustainability Focus: Eco-compatible building materials and low-emission heating systems.
  • Known for: Its "ski-in/ski-out" accessibility and panoramic mountain views.

Grand Hotel Tremezzo

An Art Nouveau icon on Lake Como that focuses on the preservation of the landscape and the support of local artisans.

Key Facts:

  • Region: Lombardy (Lake Como), Italy
  • Certification: Green Globe
  • Primary Sustainability Focus: Reduction of CO2 emissions and ethical sourcing of all boutique products.
  • Known for: Its "Water-On-The-Water" floating pool and lush century-old park.
