Le Calandre (Rubano)

Chef Massimiliano Alajmo became the youngest chef ever to receive three Michelin stars at age 28. His cooking is deeply multisensory, often encouraging guests to eat with their hands or smell specific aromas before tasting.

Famous Dishes:

  • Cuttlefish Cappuccino
  • Risotto Passi d'Oro
  • Mozzarella of Almonds

Antica Pesa (Rome)

Located in the Trastevere district, this historic restaurant dates back to the 17th century. It is a favorite of international celebrities and is known for serving Roman classics in an upscale, artistic environment.

Famous Dishes:

  • Cacio e Pepe
  • Artichoke "Alla Giudia"
  • Tiramisu Antica Pesa

Piazza Duomo (Alba)

Located in the heart of white truffle country, Chef Enrico Crippa’s three-star restaurant is famous for its "Salad 21, 31, 41, 51..." which changes based on the number of herbs and greens available in his garden that day.

Famoush Dishes:

  • Salad 21, 31, 41, 51
  • Alba White Truffle over Risotto
  • Panna Cotta Matisse
