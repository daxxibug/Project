Metropolitana (Metro Trains)

Underground rapid transit found in Rome, Milan, Naples, Turin, Brescia, Catania, and Genoa.

Tram

Rail-based streetcars. Milan operates one of the world's most famous tram networks, still using vintage "Series 1500" wooden trams from the 1920s alongside modern low-floor models.

Funicolare (Funicular)

Cable-driven cars used specifically to move people up and down steep hills in cities like Naples, Bergamo, and Genoa.

Monopattino Electtrico (E-Scooter)

Stand-up electric scooters that have become a standard part of "micro-mobility" in Italian city centers through sharing apps.
