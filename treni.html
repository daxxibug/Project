Frecciarrossa (Red Arrow)

The premier high-speed "bullet" train that reaches speed of up to 300 km/h. It is used for long distances (e.g., Milan to Rome).

Frecciargento & Frecciabianca

Slightly slower high-speed trains (200–250 km/h) that connect major cities with smaller provincial hubs.

Italo

A modern, privately-owned high-speed train that competes with the state-run "Frecce" lines.
